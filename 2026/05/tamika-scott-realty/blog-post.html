Agent Marketing Desk completed a full logo design and website build for Tamika Scott, REALTOR® with Keller Williams Realty Southwest. The result is homesbytamika.com, a clean, professional brand presence built to match Tamika's reputation as a patient, knowledgeable guide for first-time buyers and renters transitioning to homeownership in Greater Houston.

Building a Brand That Reflects the Agent

Tamika has spent nearly six years earning trust in one of Houston's most competitive real estate markets. Her approach is education-first: credit coaching, no-pressure conversations, and a personalized plan for every client. That is exactly the kind of story a brand should tell at first glance.

We designed her brand to signal professionalism, warmth, and competence to the buyers and sellers she serves across Sugar Land, Katy, Pearland, Missouri City, and the broader Greater Houston area.

The Logo

The Tamika Scott logo pairs a clean wordmark with gold accents, nodding to quality and results without feeling corporate or cold. It works across social media, email signatures, yard signs, and print. We also developed a white-on-dark version for use on photography backgrounds and dark-mode contexts.

The Website

Tamika came to us with a clear vision: she wanted a one-stop shop for anyone at any stage of the real estate process. Our team built it. Whether someone is buying, selling, or investing, the resources are there. Key sections include:

  • Home buying tips for buyers, sellers, and investors
  • Trusted lender referrals to help clients get pre-approved and ready
  • Inspector and legal resources, including attorneys and inspectors Tamika recommends personally
  • Current listings and rental properties updated directly on the site
  • Credit and readiness coaching for clients who need a clear plan before they are ready to buy
  • Positioned for AI search — every page is written in plain, specific language with clear structure so AI search assistants can read and understand what Tamika offers, where she works, and who she serves. As more buyers start their home search by asking AI tools instead of Google, having a well-structured, content-rich site puts Tamika in a stronger position to be surfaced as a relevant result
